Enjoy modern comforts and convenience in Najran

The expansive Park Inn by Radisson, Najran welcomes you to a home away from home. Situated in Najran city center, the Park Inn provides a convenient location for those on both business or leisure trips. The hotel's 107 modern rooms and suites offer guests comfort and convenience near the city center with impressive views of Najran Football Stadium. Live-Inn Restaurant serves a variety of international dishes in an all-day dining layout and also offers snacks and fresh drinks. The hotel has off-site parking, very close to the building for guests and business delegates. Our Park Inn by Radisson Hotel in Najran city center is near shopping areas, ministries, and historic attractions such as Turki Al Madi Palace, Raoum Castle, and Najran Valley Dam. After a day of exploring, relax and enjoy our hotel's many amenities, including two separate swimming pools for men and women, a fitness center, and a selection of restaurants.
